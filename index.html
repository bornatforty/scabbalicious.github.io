About Me

Who am I? How did I get here? What kind of person gives up hanging with celebrities to become a web developer?

  • Funny Intro

    I'm just a regular dude that enjoys riding a snowboard over sand dunes, speaks fluent Japanese and once drove a jeep backwards down a mountain on a single-lane pathway during an off-road challenge. But I wasn't always like this.

  • Plot Twist

    Everything changed in 2014 when I decided to quit my job as a butler to become a foreign currency day-trader. SPOILER ALERT: I wasn't too successful. But during that time I did rediscover my love of art and my passion to create things.

  • Catchy Hook

    It started with painting. And then moved to collage and large art pieces. I started painting at live shows, had pieces on display in Las Vegas galleries and started selling my artwork too. But why limit myself to the real world when I can create things in the virtual space too? And that's around the time I decided to learn front-end web development.

  • Bring It All Home

    I am a recent graduate of the Punchcode Front-End Developer bootcamp here in Las Vegas. We started with learning the fundamentals (HTML5, CSS3, JavaScript), learned more challenging variations such as jQuery, moved on to frameworks like React and Node and finally learned to tie everything together in larger projects. I learned a lot about coding and I learned a lot about myself.
